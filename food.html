I use to work in restaurants. I worked really hard, in places across the U.S. and Europe like Green Zebra, Il Bottaccio, In De Wulf and The Cakebread Ranch. I can't believe how much I learned—but I always felt like something was missing.

I'm currently exploring ways of using food to approach issues of social justice and connect with others. As a cook I'm specifically drawn to how dining and food preparation, both inside and outside our homes, intersects with issues of health and equality.

In 2015 I took a position teaching middle schoolers in public schools about food and how to cook it. Here is our blog.
